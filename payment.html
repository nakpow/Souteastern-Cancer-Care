Purchase Ticket for the event

  1. Click on the donate button and follow along the instructions.
  2. Click on the space provided for entering the amount. The ticket price 65$ per person. So Please enter the amount accordingly.
  3. After the amount is entered, click on the pay with credit card or make payment through paypal.
  4. If you are paying through credit / Debit. Please enter your credit card/Debit card Information for payment.
  5. You must print the receipt after the payment is successful and bring it along to the event.
  6. For Some reason, if you are unable to print the tickets, please arrive to the show early and see a customer representative.
